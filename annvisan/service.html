Recruitment Services

Recruitment service refers to the process provided by specialized agencies or consultants to help organizations find and hire suitable candidates for job positions.These services manage all part of the hiring process, from sourcing candidates to conducting interviews and final selection. These are the type of Recruitment service.

1.Permanent Staffing -> Permanent staffing refers to the process of hiring employees on a long-term basis for full-time positions within an organization.

2.Temporary Staffing -> Temporary staffing refers to the process of hiring employees on a short-term basis or specific project.

1.Executive Search service -> Executive Search Service involve specialized recruitment for top-level management or executive positions, such as CEOs, CFOs, or Directors.

HR Consulting Services

An HR Consultant is a professional who provides expert advice and solutions to organizations in managing their human resources effectively. They help improve HR policies, systems, and strategies to enhance employees performance, ensure legal comliance, and support overall business goals.

Contract Staffing Service

It is a type of employment arrangement where a worker is hired by a staffing agency or contractor to work for a client company for a specific period of time. The worker is not a permanent employee of the client company but rather a temporary employee who is contracted to work on a specific project or assignment.
